My Hobbies
Travelling
Traveling is more than just a pastime for me; it's a way of life. Exploring new destinations, immersing myself in diverse cultures, and uncovering the beauty of our world bring me immense joy and endless opportunities for personal growth.
Super Cars
I’m fascinated by supercars — their speed, power, and precision. I love watching drifts and drag races, learning about how these machines perform, and imagining the thrill of being behind the wheel. My ultimate dream car is the Supra MK4 with its V12 engine and 340 horsepower, a true masterpiece that combines beauty and power.
Arabic Calligraphy
Arabic calligraphy and painting are my way of slowing down and expressing myself. I love the flow of the letters, the precision of each stroke, and the beauty that emerges when art meets language. Painting lets me explore colors, shapes, and emotions, creating pieces that reflect my thoughts and imagination.
Photography
Photography is my way of capturing the world's fleeting moments and turning them into lasting memories. With a camera in hand, I explore the beauty of everyday life, seeking to frame the world in a unique and captivating way.
